Introduction to Computer Graphics

Are you fascinated by the captivating worlds of video games or the mesmerizing effects of CGI in blockbuster movies? If so, the "Introduction to Computer Graphics" course is your gateway to understanding the magic that brings these visual wonders to life. In a world driven by digital imagery, this course unravels the secrets of computer graphics. Explore the mathematics of immersive digital environments, dive into the intricacies of 3D modeling, and unlock the power of ray tracing as we unveil the art and science of computer graphics.
